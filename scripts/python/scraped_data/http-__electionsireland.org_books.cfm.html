Elections 2011

Seán Donnelly's complete guide to Dáil, Seanad and Presidential elections in Ireland in 2011, including comprehensive background and historical information, has just been published in association with ElectionsIreland.org. It is available for order now at a special introductory price of €31.50 (+p&p) - 30% off the list price.

Lulu.com provides a print-to-order service and a choice of delivery options. A preview of the table of contents and introductory pages is available from the Elections 2011 page on Lulu.com.

As well as comprehensive details of all the 2011 elections, there is a wealth of information on referendums, by elections and all general elections since 1918. Indexes cover all TDs and Senators since 1918, and all candidates who stood for the Dáil and Seanad in 2011. There is also a directory section with details of all current political parties, TDs and Senators.
