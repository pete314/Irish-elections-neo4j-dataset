1999 European Election

The Republic of Ireland elects 15 members of the European Parliament from four constituencies. Northern Ireland elects three of the UK's 87 representatives from a single constituency.

Representation from the Republic of Ireland includes 6 Fianna Fáil, 4 Fine Gael, 2 Green, 2 Independents and 1 Labour. Northern Ireland is represented by one each from DUP, SDLP and UUP.
