Government of Ireland
Official Government web site with links to various Departments and background information on the working and history of the Oireachtas

Iris Oifigiúil
The official Irish State gazette, published twice a week on Tuesdays and Fridays. Full PDF archives are available since 2002.

Acts of the Oireachtas
All legislation passed by the Oireachtas between 1922 and 2003.

Constitution of Ireland
Website of the All Party Oireachtas Committee of the Constitution. A PDF of the Constitution can be downloaded in English and Irish.

Constituency Commission (Republic of Ireland)
The Constituency Commission is established under Part 2 of the Electoral Act 1997 to review Dáil and European Parliament Constituencies. Includes detailed maps of electoral areas.

Boundary Commission (Northern Ireland)
The Boundary Commission for Northern Ireland is established under the Parliamentary Constituencies Act 1986 and the Boundary Commissions Act 1992. Includes detailed maps of constituencies.

Referendum Commission
The primary role of the Referendum Commission is to explain the subject matter of referendum proposals, to promote public awareness of the referendum and to encourage the electorate to vote at the poll.

Citizens Information
This wide-ranging website has a section that gives some details on how elections work in the Republic of Ireland.

Nicholas Whyte's Site
Information about elections in Northern Ireland since 1885 and a lot more! Includes detailed results from 1998 and 2003 Assembly Elections and Northern Ireland Local Elections back to 1973.

KildareStreet.com
A non-partisan website which aims to make it easy for people to keep tabs on their elected representatives in the Houses of the Oireachtas.

MiCandidate.eu

UK Election Results
Very comprehensive site on United Kingdom Election results, with detailed results of General Elections since 1983. Also European and Regional results, and a wealth of background detail.

Leigh Rayment's Peerage Page
This site contains a list of constituencies in Ireland and their representatives from 1692-1800, and also a list of all Westminster constituencies and their representatives from the Restoration to present day including dates of birth and death.
