27 October 2011         Candidates

Mary McAleese was installed for her second term in office at an inauguration ceremony in Dublin Castle on 11 November 2004. Under the provision of Article 12.3.3° of the Constitution, the next election for President is due between 12 August and 11 November 2011. The Minister for the Environment, Community and Local Government announced on 30 August 2011 that the Presidential Election would be held on 27 October 2011. Last day for receipt of nominations is 28 September 2011.
